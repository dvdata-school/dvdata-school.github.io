About Me

Hi, I’m Divya Rajagopal, the founder of DV Data School. This isn’t a big institute with layers of people — it’s just me, teaching you directly. My goal is simple: make data less intimidating and more exciting. I’ve walked the same path of confusion and curiosity you’re on right now, and I know exactly what it feels like to start fresh.

That’s why every course here is designed to be practical, clear, and rooted in real-world projects. Think of this as sitting down with a friend who’s obsessed with data and wants to show you how to master it too. If you’re ready to change the way you work with data, you’re in the right place.
